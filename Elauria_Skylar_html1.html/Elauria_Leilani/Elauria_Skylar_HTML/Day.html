The First Day

Febuary 19th, 2021 around 11:25 a.m. a beautiful baby girl
was born to first time parents.The first glance into her tiny
barely open eyes we fell in love.Now it was time to take her
home for the first time!As first time parents we were scared
but prepared as much as we could be! The nurses finally
released us and rolled my wife out while she was holding
our little girl. We put the car seat in and headed home
for our adventure to begin!
